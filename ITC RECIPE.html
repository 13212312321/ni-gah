RECIPE: GRILLED PORKCHOP

  • 1 kilo of Porkchop
  • Soy Sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Brown Sugar
  • 1 Clove Garlic
  • 6pcs Calamansi
  • Spring Onion

  • Fill the tupperware or a mixing bowl with a 3 cups of soy sauce and 2 cups of vinegar.
  • Add three table spoons of sugar.
  • Crush the garlic into 5 pieces and add them to the Tupperware/Mixing Bowl.
  • Slice the Calamansi and squeeze all of them to the Tupperware/Mixing Bowl.
  • Slice the porkchop to your size choice, prefferably perfect for grilling.
  • Add the sliced porkchop to the Tupperware/Mixing Bowl.
  • Mix it all together and marinate it atleast 1-2hrs or much better overnight to get that sweetness and juicyness of the porkchop.
  • After marinating, grill the porkchop at 375-400 degrees.
  • Once you are done grilling, Slice or mince the Spring onion into small pieces.
  • Once you are done, serve the grilled porkchop and top it with the minced Spring Onion.
  • Make your preferred sauce for the Porkchop.
  • Taste the Juicyness, Finger lickin good porkchop.
